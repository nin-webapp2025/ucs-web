The Mark

Ref · Identity
Primary · on Cream / White
Full-colour mark. Default for light sections. Use as-is — do not recolour.
Export pending
On Dark · Cinematic
On Midnight, place on a cream plate. A reversed / knockout export (Cloud wordmark) is still to be produced.
CC
Favicon · App Icon
Standalone “C” monogram in the brand gradient on Deep Navy / Midnight.
clear space ≥ height of “C” bowl ≥ bowl
Clear Space & Minimum Size
Keep padding on all sides ≥ the height of the “C” bowl. Never crowd the mark with type, rules, or imagery.

Do

  • Use the official mark on cream or white
  • Reverse to Cloud/white on the dark hero & footer
  • Scrim busy photos before placing the logo
  • Keep the “C” gradient direction intact

Don't

  • Recolour outside the brand blues
  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the mark
  • Add drop shadows or outlines
  • Place the full-colour mark on busy photography

Imagery

Imagery & Mood

Ref · Visual direction
Golden-hour infrastructure
Blueprint · engineering blue
BIM / GIS · digital

Lighting

Cinematic, directional. Golden-hour warmth over infrastructure; cool blueprint-blue for tech moments; soft even light on professional light sections.

Materials & Texture

Concrete, steel, glass, asphalt; blueprint & CAD line work; subtle grid / topographic patterns; matte navy surfaces with fine grain.

Motion & Camera

Slow, confident, purposeful — drone aerials, parallax on scroll, animated counters, gentle reveals. Premium restraint, never frantic.

System in Application

Ref · UI & components
Buttons & actions
Primary = signature gradient. Ghost = cyan hairline on dark. Amber reserved for a single high-emphasis moment per view. Every control names its outcome — “Request a Consultation”, not “Submit”.
